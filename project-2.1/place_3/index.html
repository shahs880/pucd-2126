Colaba Causeway

At some point it became a tradition to go jewelry shopping at Colaba Causeway at the start of every summer and winter break. Before the monsoon kicked in and the humidity cleared for a couple of days we would go with our money safely guarded. Over the years we established a routine that consisted of walking from one end to the other and back. Bargaining for lower prices for the same, if not better jewelry you now get at any Forever21. The guy managing the 4th stall to the left of the McDonalds knows us now.

Food Recommendation:
Theobroma Cafe at the end of the stretch has the best brownies in the city.
