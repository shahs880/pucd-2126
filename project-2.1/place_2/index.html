Rajabhai Tower

I pass this or at least catch a glimpse of it every time I go to South Bombay-which is at least thrice a week- and it always reminds me of my grandfather. When I was a kid my grandfather would take us to his mother’s house every Sunday. And every Sunday I would pass this clock tower and every Sunday he would point it out to me. I would crane my neck to see the top of it from inside the car and now even though I’m a lot older and definitely taller, I still crane my neck to see it from my car.

Tips:
There’s a field called Azaad Maidan across from it where people play cricket almost every weekend.
