kala ghoda

This is definitely my happy place. The narrow and entangled mess of streets hold some of my favorite places in the city. From food to art to music, this place has it all. A furniture shop with the craziest pieces, The National Gallery of Modern Art, a beautiful health-food cafe called The Pantry, Rhythm House, an icon for buying records or cassettes and several small fashion boutiques crowd these lanes. Every February there is the Kala Ghoda Arts Festival where thousands of art and craft stalls line the roads coupled with installations and performance artists.

Food Recommendation:
Mamagoto is a must. The Spicy Bangkok bowl and the Massaman curry are to die for.
