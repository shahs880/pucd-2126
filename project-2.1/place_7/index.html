Marine Drive

This is easily my favorite place in the city. I have spent endless hours either sitting by the ocean or driving by it. The summer before University my friend and I would drive here every night after picking up fro-yo or ice cream. Look out for the morons on bikes that seem to have a death wish.

Food Reccomendations:
Because it covers such a long stretch, food options depend where you are. On one end there is Cream Center, an icon for quick and delicious greasy food or Belgian Waffles which can only be described as the best thing to happen to this city since Palladium. On the other there is Salt Water Cafe or Pizza by the Bay, which has open seating with a shade.
