Kitab Khana

Around Kala Ghoda, there is a bookstore called Kitab Khana which specializes in Indian authors. From a family filled to the brim with avid readers that spent every Sunday at some sort of bookstore, Kitab Khana is new even for us. It opened a few years ago and was instantly a book lovers heaven. A small cafe in the back and the small couches on the second floor let you forget the world outside. I of course go there with my brother and grandfather. We do our own thing and sometimes bump into each other while browsing the shelves. That always makes us smile.

Tips:
The owner always recommends the best books, I discovered some interesting books and authors there.
