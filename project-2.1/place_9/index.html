Sea Link

Its a great place to drive across and the view of the city from the bridge is great. Its fairly new and for some reason I’ve been asleep 75% of the time I’ve passed it but the view is really great. You can almost see the progression of the city through the years. I remember watching a movie where the construction of this bridge was just starting. I can see the skyscrapers my friends live in from it too. And there has never once been a traffic jam on it. Which if you know Bombay, is a great and rather unique accomplishment.
