madras cafe

In the middle of December break my brother had his friends over. After a night of drinking, losing all the beer pong balls that fell off the terrace and exhausting our playlist, we had nothing to do. It was 5:30 am and so we walked in our exhausted and still slightly drunk pajama wearing state to Madras Cafe. We then proceeded to eat the best meal any of us had eaten in that restaurant. Steaming idlis and crispy dosas washed down with their special filter coffee was the best way to end the night.

Top 3 Food Reccomendations:
Appam & Stew
Raagi Dosa
Filter Coffee
