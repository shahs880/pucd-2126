NCPA

This is one of the few theaters that Bombay has to offer. I go here as often as possible. We once went on a school trip to see an orchestra from Germany perform here and it was magical. I also saw the Indian version of 12 Angry Men adapted to a play and it might have been one of the best plays I have ever seen.
