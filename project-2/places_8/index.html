saltwater cafe

When I say I love the food here, its an understatement. Everything on their menu besides the Burrata is perfect. I went there for my birthday dinner once with my family. My father might be one of the pickiest eaters out there and even he loved it! One of the handful of places that aren’t on his “we are never going to that restaurant again” list. We once went there for a friend’s birthday and then sat across the road on Marine Drive till 3:00 am after which her mother yelled at her to come home.

Their breakfast/brunch is supposed to be amazing but I have never managed to wake up early enough for that.
Curry roasted veggie burger
Poached pear salad
Asparagus ravioli
Chocolate ganache cake

Go whenever you want but Saturdays are difficult to get in. Make a reservation before you go.
