kitab khana

A bookstore specializing in Indian authors. From a family filled to the brim with avid readers that spent every Sunday at some sort of bookstore, Kitab Khana is new even for us. It opened a few years ago and was instantly a book lovers heaven. A small cafe in the back and the small couches on the second floor let you forget the world outside. I of course go there with my brother and grandfather. We do our own thing and sometimes bump into each other while browsing the shelves. That always makes us smile.

Honestly, anywhere in Kala Ghoda is a good place to start

Talk to the owner about books that he would recommend. I have discovered some pretty interesting books and authors there

Saturdays can be packed so weekdays or Sundays are perfect
